Frequently Asked Questions

Can guests make offensive songs?

No. Clean mode filters content automatically, and you can add custom banned words. Staff can also approve songs before they play.

Do we have to play everything?

No. Staff can approve, skip, or reorder songs in the queue before they play.

How fast does it generate on a busy night?

Songs typically generate in 23 minutes, even when the room is full. Requests queue cleanly so your staff stays in control and the room never feels chaotic.

Can we limit genres/volume/length?

Yes. Set genre presets, volume limits, and maximum song length in your venue settings.

What if Wi-Fi is bad?

The system works with minimal bandwidth. Songs are generated server-side and streamed efficiently.

Do guests need an app?

No. Guests just scan the QR code and use their browser. No app download required.

Who owns the songs?

You and your guests own the songs. You can reuse content for marketing with proper attribution.
